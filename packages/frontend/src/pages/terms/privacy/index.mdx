**Introduction**

BPM 2025 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from visitors to our website and participants in our conference ("you," "your"), how we use that information, and the measures we take to safeguard your data.

By accessing or using our website or participating in the conference, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

**1. Information we collect**

We may collect the following information:

- Name
- Email address
- Billing address
- Affiliation or organization
- Dietary or accessibility preferences (if voluntarily provided)

**2. How we use your information**

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

- 2.1 To register and manage your participation in the conference.
- 2.2 To communicate important updates or changes related to the event.
- 2.3 To process payments and issue invoices or receipts.
- 2.4 To improve our website and conference experience.
- 2.5 To comply with legal obligations.
- 2.6 To provide networking opportunities during the event.

**3. Sharing of information**

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

- 3.1 **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors assisting in registration, payment processing, or event logistics.
- 3.2 **Event Partners**: Co-hosts or sponsors of the conference, when necessary and relevant.
- 3.3 **Legal Authorities**: When required to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

**4. Data retention**

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal requirements.

**5. Cookies and tracking technologies**

Our website does not make use of any kind of cookie. However, our payment processor and external links might make use of cookies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties for more information about their use of cookies.

**6. Data security**

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**7. Your rights**

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- 7.1 **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data.
- 7.2 **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate information.
- 7.3 **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements.
- 7.4 **Objection**: Object to certain data uses, such as direct marketing.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details below.

**8. Third-Party links**

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

**9. Updates to this policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our website or participation in the conference signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
